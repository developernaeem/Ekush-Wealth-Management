Solutions Ekush Provides

Mutual Fund

Mutual Fund is a diversified pooled investment vehicle that provides investment solutions not only to people of all walk of life but also to large corporate. "Ekush First Unit Fund" is our first mutual fund.

Institutional Fund Management

Under the umbrella of "Separately Managed Accounts (SMA)", we provide customized investment solutions for Institutions based on client specific risk-return objctives, constraints, rules and regulations.

Structured Finance

Ekush helps corporates not only to craft their optimum financing strategies but also to implement the stratigies by arranging debt and equity finance both from local and international financers.
